What is happening???

Forests, wildlife, and natural ecosystems all around our world are rapidly degrading. Our urge for a brighter future has left our present distraught. Deforestation, pollution, and the overuse of land are destroying our planet faster than we can recover it. The actions that we have taken have deteriorated the land that once birthed us. SDG 15 aims to protect and restore these land ecosystems, but right now, they are all at an alarming threat, which is us. Understanding the cause will be the first step in fixing our world.

Cause

The destruction you can see on land is driven by the activities of us humans. Large-scale deforestation for agriculture and urban growth removes habitats in favor of our cities and farms. Pollution from industries and improper waste management harms our soil and water. Our thirst for a better future has left the present ecosystems with nothing. The overuse of resources on our land, pollution, and climate change have all brought about the changes in the world we live in today. All of these contribute to the disruption of our ecosystems, so we must find a way to stop these practices. But beyond what we see, there is damage happening silently. The loss of biodiversity weakens entire food chains, making species more vulnerable to extinction. Soil degradation from mining, chemicals, and overfarming reduces the land’s ability to grow healthy crops, pushing communities into food insecurity.
